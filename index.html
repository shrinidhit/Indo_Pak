Introducing the First National conference on India and Pakistan

Ever since the Partition in 1947, India and Pakistan's relationship has been bound by conflict. Are the Nation's futures burdened by their pasts? Our answer is a simple experiment: India's leaders, Pakistan's leaders, and young Undergraduates of the diaspora communities, all under one roof.

1.Listen

Attend keynotes and panels by leaders heavily involved in Indo-Pak relations

2.Discuss

Engage in roundtable discussions on current Indo-Pak issues

3.Reshape

Strategize for India and Pakistan's future relationship

Renowned Speakers from India and Pakistan

Syed Akbaruddin

Syed Akbaruddin is the Permanent Representative of India to the United Nations. He previously served as the Spokesperson of the Ministry of External Affairs.

Raza Rumi

Raza Rumi is a Pakistani columnist, policy analyst, and journalist. presently, he is a scholar in residence at Ithica College. Rumi had an attempted assassination attempt from the Taliban-affiliate Lashkar-e-Jhangvi in 2014.

Himanshu Suri

Himanshu Suri is an American rapper from Queens, New York, and founder of Greedhead Music. His music and work has focused on the South Asian artist and culture.
