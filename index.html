The First Collegiate Conference on India and Pakistan

Ever since the Partition in 1947, India and Pakistan’s relationship has been bound by conflict. Our goal is to talk about it. For the first time, United States’ most brilliant undergraduate students join with top Indian and Pakistani leaders to discuss Indo-Pak relations.

Bringing together undergraduates...

Network with the brightest students who are passionate about advancing Indo-Pak relations.

...to challenge preconceived notions and foster new ideas...

Engage with thought leaders in intimate settings on issues ranging from politics and economics to healthcare and sustainability

...and actually implement them.

Strategize with students across different disciplines to implement innovative and practical solutions
